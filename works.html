Tn40Stores App

Tn40Stores

An online grocery store located in Coimbatore District where Users are Authenticated through OTP in which users can purchase grocery with search, cart, ordering functionality. It also has admin module to add, update, delete products as well as recieveing orders. The recieved orders are Door Step Delivered to Customers by TN40Stores Delivery Team

Authentication

  • Easy Authentication through Mobile Phone using OTP

Functionalities

  • Searching a Product
  • Products are Categorized and Sub-Categorized
  • Adding Products to Cart
  • Editing or Deleting Products from Cart
  • Ordering Products Online (Which are Door Step Delivered by TN40Stores)

Admin

  • Add Products
  • Update Products
  • Delete Products
  • Recieving Orders Done by Users
  • History of Orders
